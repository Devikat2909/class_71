jantar mantar

I had visted in aug 2016

The Jantar Mantar is a collection of 19 astronomical instruments built by the Rajput king Sawai Jai Singh II, the founder of Jaipur, Rajasthan. The monument was completed in 1734. It features the world's largest stone sundial, and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is located near City Palace and Hawa Mahal.

  • LEGO plans to find replacment material for bricks by 2030

    June 23, 2021 Tim Barso Copenhagen (Reuters) -Lego plans to find a suitable green alternative to petroleum-based plastics and begin selling toy building bricks made from recycled PET bottles in 18-24 months. A Danish toy maker said Wednesday. It is difficult for LEGO to find alternative materials, and over the past six years, about 150 engineers and scientists have tested a variety of plant-based recycled materials. Tim Brooks, Lego’s Vice President of Environmental Responsibility, said: “We hope that not only the play that the product stimulates, but also the materials used will have a positive impact on the planet.”

First Space Encyclopaedia

Reach for the stars in the updated First Space Encyclopedia! Explore the planets and discover amazing facts about nearby galaxies. A delightful first reference book about space for young astronomers, First Space Encyclopedia takes readers on a journey through the universe, from the beginning of space itself to current space technology. Find out what it takes to be an astronaut, what it is like to live in space, and what they take with them in their suitcases! Children can test their knowledge with quizzes, try out at-home space activities, learn how to find constellations in the night sky, and see the phases of the moon. Packed full of engaging photography and easy-to-follow text, First Space Encyclopedia brings space down to Earth for curious young readers.
